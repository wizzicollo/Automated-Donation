About US

Founded by Phonix-Devs in 2020

Automated-Donations comes in to assist school girls in most sub-saharan countries, these young-turks are unable to access pads and other supplies needed for their periods. The year 2016 studies from ministry of education revealed girls from poor families miss 20 % of school days in a year due to lack of sanitary towels the data indicated that a girl in primary school between class 6 and 8 can lose up to 18 weeks out of 108 weeks while those in high school can lose almost 24 weeks out of 144 weeks of learning. We are dedicated to dealing with this problem by not only providing sanitary towels but also providing clean water and sanitation facilities such as toilets to ensure they are able to meet the guidelines for proper menstrual hygiene as defined by UNICEF. Automated-Donations enables repeat donations for donors. Our platform allows users to donate to charity/charities such as this one regularly, to enable young school girls continue with their school program with ease. We 100% automates the donation process by allowing donors to set up regular donations e.g monthly donations of stipulated amounts.
